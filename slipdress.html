Conscious Closet Library

Slip Dress

A 90s-inspired, midi-length slip dress with microflorals or soft solids. Feminine, versatile, and always effortless.

Everyday Ease

Layer with a white ribbed tee and worn-in sneakers. Top it with a denim jacket or linen button-up. Perfect for casual days with movement.

Soft Romantic

Pair with a cropped cardigan in rose or cream. Add minimal gold jewelry and leather flats. Feels like a quiet walk through a garden.

Creative Utility

Wear over a black tee or long sleeve. Top with a boxy blazer or utility jacket. Finish with loafers or boots. Ideal for creative work days.

Suggested Fabrics & Colors

  • Sand-washed silk
  • Matte satin or lightweight viscose
  • Colors: dusty rose, moss green, cream, black, microfloral prints

Style Muses

Winona Ryder (90s), Sofia Coppola, Zadie Smith
