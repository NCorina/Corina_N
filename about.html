About Me

Hi, I’m Corina Nicoara — a researcher, designer, and front-end developer exploring the intersection of aesthetics, authenticity, and digital experience. I’m currently completing a PhD in entrepreneurship, where I study how businesses navigate identity, strategic change, and design choices. Along the way, I fell in love with front-end development — not just as a technical skill, but as a form of expression and storytelling. Building this site helped me see how research, data, and design can come together to create something thoughtful, personal, and useful. This space is where I bring it all together: • A wardrobe library where style becomes narrative and system • A creative archive of digital art and visual inspiration • A portfolio of research and data work, grounded in rigorous methods and curiosity • A styling & design philosophy rooted in mindfulness and intentionality I love designing for beauty and meaning—whether I’m visualizing data, teaching strategic thinking, or styling a silk skirt. I believe great design isn’t just about how it looks, but how it feels, how it flows, and how it reflects what matters. Thanks for being here.
