About Me

About Me Hi, I’m Corina Nicoara — a Ph.D. Candidate passionate about exploring the intersection of authenticity, entrepreneurship, and digital expression. I’m currently completing a Ph.D. in entrepreneurship, where I study how businesses navigate identity, strategic change, and meaning-making in dynamic environments. My work is grounded in a deep curiosity about what makes ventures feel genuine — and how authenticity can be shaped, challenged, and communicated through design, technology, and narrative. I’m especially interested in bridging the gap between science, tech, and the arts. To me, research is not just an analytical pursuit but also a creative one — a space where rigor and imagination meet. This site reflects that perspective: • A portfolio of research and data work, grounded in rigorous methods and curiosity • A statement of research and design interests • A wardrobe library where style becomes narrative and system • A creative archive of digital art and visual inspiration I love designing for finding truth, beauty and meaning—whether I’m visualizing data, studying entreprenurial identity or teaching strategic thinking, building interfaces or styling a silk skirt. I believe great design isn’t just about how it looks, but how it feels, how it flows, and how it reflects what matters. My aim is to bring care, clarity, and creativity to the work. Thanks for visiting.
