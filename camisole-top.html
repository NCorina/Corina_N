Fitted Camisole Top

Three Ways to Style It

Parisian Light

1. Parisian Light

Layer under a linen blazer with vintage jeans and a silk scarf. Add minimal gold jewelry.

90s Cool

2. 90s Cool

Wear over a slim white tee with cargo trousers and low-top sneakers. Finish with a claw clip.

Soft Layering

3. Soft Layering

Style under a sheer cardigan with a maxi skirt and ballet flats. Add a soft tote for texture.

Inspiration

“She was wearing a slip of a blouse, the kind that makes you feel like summer might be permanent.” — *From a forgotten novel, but never a forgotten moment*

Conscious Brand Recommendations

  • Reformation – Great for fitted linen camisoles with a soft structure, made in Los Angeles.
  • Amour Vert – Organic basics in soft neutrals. Ethically made.
  • Rouje – French-girl style camisoles, often in deadstock silk and vintage prints.
